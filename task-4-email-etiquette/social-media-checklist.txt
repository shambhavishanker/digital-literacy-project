Do’s:
Think before posting and consider how it may affect your digital image
Keep personal information private and secure
Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
Verify information before sharing it online
Maintain a positive and respectful online presence


Don’ts:
Don’t share passwords, OTPs, or sensitive personal data
Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown attachments
Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
Don’t overshare personal details publicly
Don’t spread misinformation without checking facts
